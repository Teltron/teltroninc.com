Our Clients

Teltron enjoys a varied customer base ranging from small offices with just a few telephones to entities with several hundred phones – some with single offices and others with multiple locations.

We support customers throughout east central Missouri ranging from the “Highway 47 Corridor” (St. Clair, Union, Washington, Warrenton, Troy) eastward to St. Charles County and the St. Louis metro area.

Although this is our prime coverage area we have customers as far west as Rolla and Booneville and can also provide service in Illinois’ metro east area.
